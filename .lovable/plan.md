
# Tori Speciality Dental Clinic — Website Plan

## Design System
- **Colors:** White background, soft teal/blue (#0EA5E9, #14B8A6) accents, light grey sections, dark text
- **Typography:** Clean, modern sans-serif with excellent readability
- **Style:** Rounded buttons, soft shadows, calming dental aesthetic
- **Layout:** Sticky header, floating "Call Now" button on mobile, consistent footer across all pages

---

## Page 1: Home Page
- **Hero section** with clinic tagline, background image placeholder, and two CTA buttons: "Book Appointment" and "Call Now"
- **Welcome message** — short paragraph about 24/7 care and patient comfort
- **Key Services grid** — 5 highlighted services (Emergency Care, Pediatric Dentistry, Orthodontics, Root Canal, Cosmetic Dentistry) with icons and brief descriptions
- **Why Choose Us** — 4-column section: clean clinic, compassionate doctors, modern equipment, 24/7 availability
- **Testimonials preview** — 3 rotating/scrolling 5-star patient reviews
- **Contact CTA banner** — "Ready to book?" with phone number and appointment button

## Page 2: About Us
- Mission & Vision statements
- Patient-centered care philosophy description
- Doctor profiles for Dr. Bethlehem (Dr. Beti) and Dr. Amde with placeholder photos and bios
- Section on hygiene standards and modern technology

## Page 3: Services Page
- 8 service cards/sections with detailed descriptions for: General Dentistry, Pediatric Dentistry, Orthodontics, Root Canal, Wisdom Tooth Extraction, Zirconium Crowns, Cosmetic Dentistry, 24/7 Emergency Care
- Each includes benefits, procedure overview, when to seek treatment, and a "Book Appointment" CTA

## Page 4: Testimonials Page
- 8-10 patient testimonial cards with star ratings, patient names (First Name + Last Initial), and review text
- Covers themes: friendly staff, painless procedures, emergency care, children's care, professionalism

## Page 5: Blog Page
- Blog listing page with 7 hardcoded sample articles (the topics you listed)
- Category filter tabs: Oral Health Tips, Pediatric Dentistry, Cosmetic Dentistry, Emergency Care, Orthodontics
- Each post has a featured image placeholder, title, excerpt, date, and category tag
- Individual blog post pages with full article content, social sharing buttons, and "Book Appointment" CTA
- SEO-optimized content written into each article

## Page 6: Contact Page
- Embedded Google Map for "2R23+73 Addis Ababa"
- Clinic address, click-to-call phone link, 24/7 availability badge
- Simple appointment request form (Name, Phone, Email, Message) with validation — shows success toast on submit
- Emergency contact CTA section

---

## Global Components
- **Sticky header** with clinic logo/name, navigation links (Home, About, Services, Testimonials, Blog, Contact), and "Book Appointment" button
- **Mobile hamburger menu** with smooth transitions
- **Floating "Call Now" button** on mobile screens
- **Footer** with quick links, contact info, hours (24/7), and social media icon placeholders
- **SEO meta tags** on every page with relevant dental keywords for Addis Ababa

---

## Technical Notes
- All content is hardcoded (no backend needed initially)
- Placeholder images throughout — you can upload real clinic/doctor photos after the build
- Contact form shows a success message on submit (no email sending — that can be added later)
- Fully responsive across desktop, tablet, and mobile
- Accessible design with proper contrast, alt text, and semantic HTML
